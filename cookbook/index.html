Swedish Meatballs

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • ground pork
  • panko bread crumbs
  • yellow onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • ground allspice
  • ground nutmeg
  • eggs
  • garlic cloves
  • butter

Directions

  1. mix ingredients
  2. form balls
  3. sear meatballs in butter
  4. bake meatballs to finish cooking
  5. make gravy
  6. serve with mashed potatoes and gravy

Japanese Curry

Ingredients

  • curry block
  • yellow onion
  • beef chunks/ground beef
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. sear beef, set aside
  2. saute onions and garlic. add in carrots and potatoes
  3. add water and bring to boil before lowering heat
  4. Simmer and and add in curry blocks
  5. serve over fresh rice

Pasta with Red Sauce

Ingredients

  • favorite brand of pasta sauce
  • favorite brand of pasta
  • yellow onion
  • italian sausage
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. bring water to boil, heavily salt, cook pasta to one minute under instruction time
  2. saute onions and garlic. add in uncased sausage
  3. add pasta sauce and a little bit of the pasta water to onions, garlic, and sausage
  4. drain pasta, pour sauce over pasta and stir in butter, let residule heat finish cooking to al dente
  5. grate fresh parmesan cheese over and serve
